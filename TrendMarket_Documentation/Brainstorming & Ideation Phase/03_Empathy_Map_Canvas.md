## 1. Introduction
The Empathy Map Canvas helps the team step directly into the shoes of the target user—the online shopper. By mapping what they see, say, do, hear, think, and feel, we align the TrendMarket development roadmap directly with user values and pain points.

---

## 2. Empathy Map Canvas Details

### 2.1 Who are we empathizing with?
* We are empathizing with **Riya**, a 24-year-old fashion enthusiast and busy professional who does 90% of her shopping online. She expects modern, fast, and secure checkout experiences.

### 2.2 What do they need to DO?
* Browse a curated catalog of fashion products.
* Add items to the cart instantly and review shipping costs.
* Pay securely using mock payment methods with instant feedback.
* Track order history and check profile details.

### 3.1 What do they SEE?
* Clean product cards displaying images, pricing, ratings, and real-time inventory counts.
* Interactive cart status counts incrementing in the header.
* Clear input validation indicators on shipping forms.
* Smooth responsive layout on both desktop and mobile screens.

### 3.2 What do they SAY?
* "I want to see what is trending immediately without scrolling through pages of unrelated items."
* "Is my payment details secure here? I don't want my credentials stolen."
* "The site is so fast, I can complete my order during my short work break."

### 3.3 What do they DO?
* Use search filters to narrow products to a specific category.
* Create accounts and update user profile info.
* Add items to the cart and proceed to mock payment pages.
* Track order histories in a unified user dashboard.

### 3.4 What do they HEAR?
* Friends saying, "I hate when e-commerce websites let you order something only to email you later saying it was out of stock."
* Technology influencers recommending websites that use secure, modern backend designs.
* Coworkers sharing stories of fast shipping and clean e-commerce platforms.

## 4. Pains & Gains

### 4.1 Pains (What frustrates them?)
* **Sluggish Interactions:** Slow page loading or delays when adding to cart.
* **Invisible Out-of-Stock Status:** Finding out an item is out of stock *after* trying to place an order.
* **Session Expirations:** Having to log back in repeatedly because the website forgot the login session.

### 4.2 Gains (What satisfies them?)
* **Seamless Experience:** Single-click catalog browsing to cart, checkout, and order completion.
* **Security Assurances:** Knowing their profile is safe due to secure session handling (JWT-in-cookie).
* **Instant Confirmation:** Receiving immediate order success statuses and viewing past receipts instantly.
